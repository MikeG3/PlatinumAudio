Why Choose Platinum Audio?


1. Extensive Education:

Platinum Audio boasts extensive education in engineering and hands-on technical expertise, ensuring top-tier service.


2. Industry Experience:

With experience in both electronic manufacturing and repairs, Platinum Audio provides unparalleled insight and skill.


3. People Over Profits:

At Platinum Audio, clients' satisfaction and care come before profits, reflecting a commitment to excellence.


4. Meticulous Craftsmanship:

Every detail matters. Platinum Audio restores and undoes damage caused by previous technicians, DIY attempts, and rushed repairs.


5. Premium Parts:

Only the best parts from reputable sources are used to ensure longevity and performance.


6. Comprehensive Documentation:

Platinum Audio provides full consultations, receipts for parts, and detailed documentation, including pictures and videos of all work performed.


7. Rigorous Testing:

Each piece of equipment is tested, double-checked, and retested, with Platinum Audio treating all equipment with the same care given to personal items.


8. Follow-Up Care:

Platinum Audio follows up after a week, a month, and even years later to ensure lasting satisfaction and performance.


9. Amplifier Expertise:

Recognizing the value of quality equipment, Platinum Audio ensures every amplifier performs optimally and remains reliable for years to come. Whether it's resolving sound cut out and scratchy sounding controls, channel imbalances, eliminating noise and distortion, loud cracks and popping sounds or restoring functionality, Platinum Audio is dedicated to making every piece shine like new or better.
