About Platinum Audio

Platinum Audio was founded in 2014 by Michael Garner, a passionate engineer, musician, and multi-disciplinary technician. Over the years, Michael has combined his deep technical expertise with a lifelong love for music and audio equipment, creating a professional service dedicated to audio restoration and innovation.



At Platinum Audio, every step of the process—from communication and troubleshooting to repairs and servicing—is handled directly by founder Michael Garner. With a Bachelor of Engineering, a DEP in Electro-Mechanics, collegial studies in engineering technologies, and a certified soldering qualification, Michael brings over 10 years of experience in commercial electronic manufacturing, 5+ years in technical support and solutions, and more than a decade of expertise in vintage audio repairs. Driven by a lifelong passion for music and perfection, Michael is dedicated to delivering exceptional audio restoration and repair services.

From Childhood Curiosity to Professional Expertise

As a young child, Michael had a clock radio with an AM tuner that he quickly learned to tune to Montreal's local oldies station, listening to music every night. Ever since that day, he was hooked. A little older, around the time CDs became the dominant music format, Michael received his first 90s-style boombox. While it provided joy, it lacked the sound quality and visual appeal of his father’s silver-faced receiver, with its mesmerizing power meters and the rumbling bass of acoustic research speakers. These speakers filled their suburban home with the sounds of Beethoven, echoing through every room.


As a teenager, Michael finally convinced his mother to let him play her flamenco guitar, a treasured instrument that had captivated him since childhood. He began taking lessons at 16 and has continued to explore music ever since. By his twenties, with a modest paycheck, he was able to invest in guitar gear and upgraded stereo systems. However, nothing could fully replicate the live music experience or the sound of his favorite gear.


Finding a Passion in Vintage Audio

With the rise of Craigslist and eBay, Michael began exploring vintage audio equipment, discovering that gear from his father’s era offered the best value and unmatched quality. Although these treasures often came with technical challenges, this coincided perfectly with the start of his technical studies. By the time he acquired his first Marantz 2230 receiver, Michael had developed enough skills to bring the equipment back to life, uncovering its unique and captivating sound.


Over the years, Michael completed a technical degree and an engineering degree, gaining experience in electronic manufacturing, quality control, and repair. Working alongside an inspiring Swiss engineer, he honed his skills in meticulous electronic restoration and manufacturing. His journey has included restoring dozens of vintage receivers from renowned brands like Marantz, Pioneer, Sansui, McIntosh, and Kenwood, always seeking the perfect sound.


A Professional Pursuit

Today, Platinum Audio stands as a testament to Michael’s lifelong passion and technical expertise. Whether restoring vintage audio equipment, repairing modern systems, or creating custom solutions, Michael combines precision craftsmanship with a deep love for music. His services have expanded beyond Montreal, reaching clients across Canada and internationally.
