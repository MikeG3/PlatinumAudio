Welcome to Platinum Audio


Platinum Audio is a Professional Audio and Electronics Lab, we provides audio restoration, repair and upgrade services


Experience working on hundreds of pieces of audio equipment, including most Midfi / Hifi brands:

Pioneer, Marantz, Sansui, Mcintosh, Luxman, Quad, Accuphase, NAD, Fisher, Rotel, Sony, Harman Kardon, Rotel, Telefunken, JVC, Toshiba, Technics, Panasonic, Hitachi, Phillips..

Our Services

  • Troubleshooting and repair of audio equipment
  • Power and Distortion Analysis of Audio Amplifiers and Sources
  • Component replacement & Modern component integration
  • Light and LED Replacement and LED conversion
  • Calibrations (Bias, DC offset, Voltage/Current supplies…)
  • Recaps: capacitor replacement and retrofitting with modern audio-grade capacitors
  • Transistor, Opamps, and semiconductor upgrades
  • Cleaning Controls: Volume, tone and balance pots, switches
  • Faceplate and accessory polishing
  • Soldering and circuit board inspection and repair
  • Frequency/channel linearity tests

Why Choose Platinum Audio?

At PlatinumAudio, we believe in offering more than just repairs—we offer an experience rooted in expertise, dedication, and a genuine passion for quality craftsmanship. Here’s why you should trust us with your prized audio equipment:


  1. Expertise You Can Trust

    With an extensive education in engineering and hands-on technical training, I bring both theory and practice together to deliver precision and reliability in every project. My background in electronic manufacturing and repairs ensures that your equipment is handled by a professional who understands it inside and out. Whether it’s a delicate vintage amplifier or cutting-edge technology, I have the skills to restore it to peak performance.


  2. People Over Profits

    My approach to business is simple: people over profits. Every piece of equipment I work on is treated with the utmost care and respect. I never rush through a job, and I’m not interested in cutting corners for the sake of speed or cost. My priority is providing you with the highest quality service, ensuring that your equipment performs better than ever—without compromise.


  3. Meticulous Attention to Detail

    I am meticulous about every aspect of my work. From restoring equipment to undoing damage done by previous technicians or DIY repairs, I ensure that no detail is overlooked. I believe that the smallest mistake can make a huge difference, and I take pride in doing things the right way—every time. Every repair, no matter how big or small, is done with precision.


  4. Only the Best Parts

    Your equipment deserves the best parts, and that’s exactly what I use. I source all components from reputable suppliers to ensure that your equipment is restored with parts that not only work but will stand the test of time. Quality matters, and I won’t settle for anything less.


  5. Full Transparency and Documentation

    You’ll always know exactly what’s going on with your equipment. I provide full consultations, receipts for all parts, and comprehensive documentation of the work done. From pictures and videos to detailed explanations of repairs, I ensure that you're fully informed every step of the way.


  6. Thorough Testing and Re-Testing

    I don’t just repair equipment—I test, double-check, and re-test everything to ensure flawless operation. I treat every piece of gear like it was my own, as I do with my personal collection. Whether it’s an audio amplifier, vintage stereo, or home theater system, I ensure it’s performing at its best before returning it to you.


  7. Ongoing Support and Follow-Up

    Your satisfaction matters to me long after the job is done. I don’t just repair your equipment and walk away—I follow up regularly to ensure everything is functioning optimally. Whether it’s a week, a month, or even years later, I’ll be there to answer questions and ensure your gear continues to meet your expectations.


  8. Restoring the Glory of Your Gear

    If you have a glorious amplifier, you're one of the lucky few who truly understands the value of top-tier audio equipment. But owning quality gear also means taking care of it. I’ll help your equipment perform like it’s brand new—ensuring that no matter how old or damaged it may be, it will continue to serve you for years to come.


  9. Restoring Your Sound to Its Full Potential

    I understand the frustration of sound cut-outs, imbalanced volume levels, noise, and distortion that plague even the most beautiful equipment. Don’t let your prized gear sit collecting dust because it’s not performing at its best. Let me restore it to its full potential, so you can enjoy clear, high-fidelity sound once again.


The PlatinumAudio Promise:

At PlatinumAudio, we don’t just fix problems—we restore the heart and soul of your audio equipment. With unmatched dedication, experience, and a personal commitment to excellence, I ensure your gear performs at its peak, so you can enjoy the sound quality it was designed to deliver.

Trust PlatinumAudio—where passion for sound meets precision and care.
